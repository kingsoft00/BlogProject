Lychee is the sole member of the genus Litchi in the soapberry family, Sapindaceae.

Mango, Mangifera indica, is an evergreen tree in the family Anacardiaceae grown for its edible fruit.

The sapodilla tree is a slow-growing evergreen that can reach heights of 100 feet.

Jackfruit, the Moraceae plant family, is an exotic fruit grown in tropical regions. It is native to South India.

Annona glabra is a tropical fruit tree in the family Annonaceae, in the same genus as the soursop and cherimoya.

The Common Guava, Yellow Guava, or Lemon Guava, grows 30 to 40 feet tall. It is widely grown in tropical and sub-tropical regions

Passiflora edulis, known as passion fruit, is a vine species of passion flower native to southern Brazil through Paraguay and northern Argentina

The papaya, papaw, or pawpaw is the plant Carica papaya, one of the 22 accepted species in the genus Carica of the family Caricaceae.

Myrciaria dubia, known as camu camu, cacari, or camocamo, is a small bushy riverside tree from the Amazon rainforest in Peru and Brazil.
